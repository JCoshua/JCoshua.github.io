Dead Man Jack

Welcome to the brutal Underworld!

Monsters roam free here. Lead by their unrelenting master, Big Frank the King of Monsters!

It is here that Dead Man Jack has come to claim the throne from Big Frank. Separating his heart from his own body to give himself immortality, he plans to weave hordes of Big Frank's undead minions to claim the title. With his unique fighting skill, and the freshly opened hole in his chest, he's able to use the abilities of the enemies he grabs and swallows against their own allies.

Fight like your afterlife depends on it!

Mike And Chip

MikeAndChip is a SciFi/Neon platformer where you perform jumps as well as use your companion Chip to navigate the game. You use him to help gain high, travel further distances and unlock areas and doors. In the game you are Mike and your companion Chip together yall are the best IT guys in the business and yall have been sent into the Computer to gather the floppy disks that contain the anti Virus and save the computer.
